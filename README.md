# 🛒 Sales Performance Dashboard
## Superstore Retail Analysis | Excel | SQL | Power BI

---

## 📌 Project Overview
Analyzed 9,993 rows of retail sales data to identify 
key revenue drivers, profit trends, and business 
opportunities across regions, categories, and customer segments.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
A retail company wanted to understand:
- Which regions are most/least profitable?
- Which product categories need attention?
- What are the seasonal sales trends?
- Which customer segment is most valuable?

---

## 📊 Dataset
- **Source:** Superstore Sales Dataset
- **Rows:** 9,993
- **Columns:** 17
- **Period:** 2015 - 2018

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Regional Performance
- West region leads with **$725K revenue** and **14.94% margin**
- Central region has critical **7.92% profit margin** — needs attention

### 2️⃣ Category Analysis  
- Technology: Best margin at **17.4%**
- Furniture: Alarming **2.55% margin** — near loss situation

### 3️⃣ Monthly Trends
- Peak months: **September, November, December**
- Slow months: **January, February**
- Revenue grew from **$484K (2015)** to **$733K (2018)**

### 4️⃣ Customer Segments
- Home Office: Highest margin **14.05%**
- Consumer: Largest base with **409 customers**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
1. Implement discount cap in Central region → **+5% margin**
2. Restructure Furniture category pricing → **+8% profitability**
3. Launch Q1 promotions for slow months → **+12% Q1 revenue**
4. Expand Home Office segment → **maintain 14% margin**
